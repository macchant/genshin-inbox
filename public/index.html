Common Questions
Where do I find my UID?
Tap your avatar in-game — your UID is right there below your name and level.
How long until I get a reply?
Usually within 24 hours. We'll try to get back to you as soon as possible.
Can I check my old messages?
Yep! Just enter your UID on the inbox page and all your messages will show up.
What should I include?
Be as detailed as you can — what happened, where it happened, and what you expected. Screenshots help too!
Is my info safe?
We only use your in-game name and UID to find your account. Nothing else stored.

Got an issue?

Send us a message and we'll sort it out. No stress!
