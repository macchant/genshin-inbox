Frequently Asked Questions
How do I find my UID?
Open your game profile by tapping your avatar. Your UID is displayed below your name and level.
How long until I get a response?
We typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent issues, please be detailed in your message.
Can I check my previous requests?
Yes! Enter your UID on the inbox page to see all your submissions and responses.
What should I include in my message?
Be as specific as possible. Include what happened, where it happened (quest name, location), and what you expected.
Is my information safe?
Absolutely. We only use your in-game name and UID to identify your account. No personal data is stored.
